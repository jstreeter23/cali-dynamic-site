About Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a major city located in southern California. Known for its entertainment industry, LA is classified as an urban city.

  • Population: 3.9 million
  • Year Incorporated: 1850
  • Region: Southern California
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income Level: $98,000 which is higher than the state average $73,000

Interesting Facts

Los Angeles is the center of the nation's film and television industry.
