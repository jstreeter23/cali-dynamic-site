About Sacramento

Sacramento is the capital city of California, located in the northern part of the state. It was incorporated in 1850 and is classified as an urban city.

  • Population: 500,000
  • Year Incorporated: 1850
  • Region: Northern California
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income Level: $91,000 which ishigher than the state average of $73,000

Interesting Facts

Sacramento is known for its Gold Rush history and as a major agricultural hub in the state.
