About San Diego

San Diego is a coastal city located in southern California, near the border with Mexico. It is classified as an urban city.

  • Population: 1.4 million
  • Year Incorporated: 1850
  • Region: Southern California
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income Level: $73,000 which is comparable to the state average of $73,000

Interesting Facts

San Diego is known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant marine culture.
